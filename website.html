AAP

A relatively new initiative, the Academic Assistance Programme has been a huge success in helping the students of BITS Goa grasp concepts, cover difficult problems and achieve excellence in academic courses. The initiative empowers students to ask doubts and encounter problems under the guidance of highly motivated student mentors
